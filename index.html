I am a PhD Student at the Faculty of Information, University of Toronto advised by Priyank Chandra and Negin Dahya. My research interests lie at the crossroads of HCI and social justice. In particular, I'm interested in critically evaluating competing values that govern technologies, and in examining ways of integrating a wide range of values and interests that empower the marginalized in technology design and development.

Before UofT, I was fortunate to work with Joyojeet Pal, Nithya Sambasivan, Rajesh Veeraraghavan, Vivek Srinivasan, and Vinodkumar Prabhakaran. I graduated with a M.A. in Regulatory Governance from Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai, India in 2016, and a B.com (Hons.) from Shri Ram College of Commerce, Delhi, India in 2014. For more details, check my CV or feel free to email me.
